Welcome to my digital garden! 

I've decided to start documenting my learnings, patch notes, and technical deep dives here. This is less formal than a blog and more of a space for me to cultivate ideas over time.

## Why a Digital Garden?

1. **Learning in Public**: Explaining concepts tests your own understanding.
2. **Reference Bank**: It's a great place to store snippets or workflows I'll need again.
3. **Tracking Progress**: Seeing a timeline of explored topics is extremely fulfilling.

I built this with Astro because of its exceptional static-site generation capabilities.
